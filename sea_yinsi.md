We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information, and the rights you have when using the app.

### 1. Information We Collect
- Account information: email or third‑party account identifiers for login and account management.
- Device information: device type, OS version, app version, language settings, and push token (for notifications).
- Usage information: feature usage, crash logs, and performance data (to improve the experience).
- User content: letters, replies, or feedback you submit in the app (displayed anonymously).

### 2. How We Use Information
- Provide core features (post, salvage, reply, react, translate, etc.).
- Account and security management (authentication, risk control, abuse prevention).
- Improve the product (performance optimization, issue diagnostics, iterations).
- Send necessary service notifications (e.g., reply or reaction alerts).

### 3. Sharing and Disclosure
- We do not sell your personal information.
- We may share information only in these cases:  
  - With service providers needed to deliver the service (e.g., cloud storage, push, payments, analytics), under strict confidentiality obligations.  
  - When required by law or to protect our legal rights.

### 4. Data Storage and Security
- We use industry‑standard security measures (encrypted transmission, access control, etc.).  
- No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we continuously improve our protections.

### 5. Your Rights
- You can access, correct, or delete your personal information.  
- You may delete your account in the app or contact us for assistance.

### 6. Children’s Privacy
- This app is not intended for children under 13. If we learn such data was collected, we will delete it.

### 7. Policy Updates
- We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in the app or on this page.

### 8. Contact Us
- If you have privacy questions, contact us via in‑app feedback or email.
