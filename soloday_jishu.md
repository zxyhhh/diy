## Frequently Asked Questions

### Where is my data stored?

SoloDay stores your daily intentions, reflections, Do items, Future notes, and preferences. Your personal records are stored locally and can sync across your devices through iCloud. If iCloud storage is full, you are not signed in, or sync is temporarily unavailable, some data may remain only on the current device.

### Will my data remain after deleting the app?

If your data has not been successfully synced to iCloud, deleting the app may also remove the local data on that device. Before deleting SoloDay, please make sure iCloud is available or export your past intentions as a backup.

### Why are weather or moon details not showing?

Weather information requires location permission and a network connection. Please check that:

- SoloDay has permission to access your location
- Your network connection is available
- Location Services are enabled in system settings
- You have tried reopening the app after a short while

Moon phase information is usually calculated on device. If it does not appear, please try restarting the app or updating to the latest version.

### Why is iCloud sync not updating immediately?

iCloud sync is handled by the system in the background and may be affected by network status, iCloud storage, Low Power Mode, and system scheduling. Please check that:

- You are signed in to iCloud
- iCloud Drive is available
- Your iCloud storage is not full
- Your devices are using the same Apple ID
- iCloud access for SoloDay has not been disabled

### Why are the focus timer, Lock Screen, or Dynamic Island states not appearing?

Please make sure your device and system version support these features, and check whether notifications and Live Activities are enabled in system settings. Some features may only be available on iPhone or newer iOS versions.

### How can I export my records?

You can export your past intentions from SoloDay's Settings page. Exported content can be used for backup or personal review.

### Does SoloDay include ads or social features?

No. SoloDay does not include social feeds, leaderboards, streak pressure, or in-app advertising.

## Privacy And Data

SoloDay is designed with privacy in mind. Your personal records belong to you. The app does not publicly display your daily intentions, tasks, future notes, or reflections to anyone else.

The weather feature may use your device location to provide local weather. Location information is used only to show relevant weather, sunrise and sunset, UV, and other daily context.

## Feedback

If you would like SoloDay to support new themes, export formats, reminder options, or if you notice any issue in the experience, please contact support. We read feedback carefully and continue improving SoloDay in future versions.

Thank you again for using SoloDay. May it be a quiet little canvas you can return to each day.
