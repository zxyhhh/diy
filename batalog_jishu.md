# BetaLog Technical Support

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Thank you for using BetaLog. BetaLog is a climbing record app designed to help climbers import or capture climbing videos, organize routes by gym, grade, color, and result, review attempts, use AI and Smart Scan features, back up media, and share selected route videos with climbing partners.

## How to Get Help

If you need support, please use the built-in support channels in the app:

- Open **BetaLog > Settings > Feedback Wall** to submit issues, ideas, or feature requests.
- Open **BetaLog > Settings > Contact Us** to join the BetaLog community on Reddit: [r/betalog](https://www.reddit.com/r/betalog/).
- Email the BetaLog support team at [zxyhdream@gmail.com](mailto:zxyhdream@gmail.com).

When reporting a problem, include your device model, operating system version, BetaLog app version, sign-in method, and a short description of what happened. Screenshots or screen recordings are helpful when the issue involves video import, AI analysis, upload status, subscriptions, or partner sharing.

## Common Support Topics

### Account and Sign-In

BetaLog supports account sign-in through supported identity providers such as Apple and Google. If you cannot sign in, confirm that your network is available, your device time is correct, and the selected sign-in provider is working. Logging out and signing in again can refresh account status without deleting your records.

### Video Import, Camera, and Photo Library

BetaLog may ask for camera, microphone, and photo library permissions so you can capture climbing videos, import existing videos, generate thumbnails, and manage route media. If import or recording does not work, check your system permission settings and confirm that BetaLog can access the selected media.

### Uploads, AI Analysis, and Smart Scan

BetaLog can upload compressed videos for cloud backup and analysis. Upload and AI analysis require a stable network connection. If a task is pending or analyzing for longer than expected, keep the app connected to the network and check **Settings > Upload History** for current status.

### Media Storage

BetaLog includes media storage management and cache clearing tools. Clearing cache removes local temporary files but does not remove records that are stored in your account. Review the in-app storage manager before freeing device space.

### BetaLog Pro and Purchases

BetaLog Pro unlocks higher limits for features such as video import, batch import, AI analysis, partner video sharing, and cloud storage. If a purchase or restore does not appear immediately, make sure you are signed in with the correct App Store or Google Play account, then use the restore option in the app. Store billing and refunds are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play according to their policies.

### Privacy and Data

BetaLog uses account information, climbing records, gym data, media files, thumbnails, and related metadata to provide recording, playback, sync, partner sharing, and analysis features. You can manage device permissions in system settings and review BetaLog's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy in the app.

## Service Availability

Some features depend on third-party services, including authentication providers, cloud hosting, storage, maps or gym information, app store billing, and AI/media processing. Temporary service interruptions or network restrictions may affect sign-in, uploads, analysis, purchases, or feedback submission.
