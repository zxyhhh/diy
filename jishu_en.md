### Data & Privacy

#### Q: Where is my data stored?
**A:** All your data is stored locally on your device. LifeGo does not transmit any information to external servers or cloud services. Your data never leaves your device.

#### Q: Is my data secure?
**A:** Yes. Since all data stays on your device, your data security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:
- Setting up a device lock (password, PIN, or biometric)
- Enabling the app's passcode feature for additional protection
- Regularly backing up your data using the export feature
- Keeping your device's operating system updated

#### Q: Can I set a password to protect the app?
**A:** Yes! You can enable passcode protection in Settings > Privacy & Security. This will require a 4-digit code every time you open the app.

âš ï¸ **Important**: Once set, the passcode cannot be recovered or reset. If you forget it, you will lose access to the app and your data.

### Data Backup & Transfer

#### Q: How do I back up my data?
**A:** To back up your data:
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Tap **Data Backup**
3. Select **Export Data**
4. Choose where to save the backup file (e.g., Files, iCloud, Google Drive)

The app will create a JSON file containing all your data.

#### Q: How do I restore my data on a new device?
**A:** To restore your data:
1. Install LifeGo on your new device
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Data Backup**
4. Select **Import Data**
5. Choose the backup file you previously exported

âš ï¸ **Important**: Make sure to export your data before switching devices. We cannot recover lost data.

#### Q: What happens to my data if I uninstall the app?
**A:** All data will be permanently deleted when you uninstall the app. Make sure to export your data before uninstalling if you want to keep it.

#### Q: Can I sync data across multiple devices?
**A:** LifeGo does not offer automatic cloud sync to protect your privacy. However, you can manually transfer data between devices using the export/import feature.

### Troubleshooting

#### Q: The app won't open or crashes on startup
**A:** Try these steps:
1. Force close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your device
3. Make sure you're running the latest version of the app
4. If you recently set a passcode, try entering it again
5. Check if you have enough storage space on your device
6. Reinstall the app (âš ï¸ Export data first!)

If the problem persists, contact support with:
- Device model and operating system version
- App version
- Description of what happens when you try to open the app

#### Q: Reminders are not working
**A:** Check the following:
1. Ensure notification permissions are enabled for LifeGo in your device settings
2. Check that the reminder toggle is ON in the specific record
3. Verify the reminder date/time is set correctly
4. Make sure "Do Not Disturb" mode isn't blocking notifications
5. On iOS, check Settings > Notifications > LifeGo
6. On Android, check Settings > Apps > LifeGo > Notifications

#### Q: I can't export my data
**A:** Try these solutions:
1. Make sure you have granted storage/file access permissions
2. Check if you have enough free space on your device
3. Try saving to a different location (e.g., Downloads folder)
4. Close other apps that might be using storage
5. Restart your device and try again

#### Q: The app is running slowly
**A:** Performance tips:
1. Restart the app
2. Clear cache by restarting your device
3. Make sure your device has sufficient free storage (at least 500MB recommended)
4. Close other running apps
5. Update to the latest app version
6. If you have a very large amount of data, consider archiving old records

#### Q: I forgot my passcode
**A:** Unfortunately, passcodes cannot be recovered or reset for security reasons. If you forget your passcode:
- You will need to uninstall and reinstall the app
- All data will be lost unless you have a backup file
- If you have a backup, you can import it after reinstalling

**Prevention**: We strongly recommend:
- Writing down your passcode in a secure place
- Creating regular data backups
- Using a memorable 4-digit code

#### Q: Text is too small/large
**A:** The app follows your device's text size settings. To adjust:
- **iOS**: Settings > Display & Brightness > Text Size
- **Android**: Settings > Display > Font size
