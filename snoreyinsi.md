# SnoreLog Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 27, 2026  
Effective Date: February 27, 2026

Welcome to SnoreLog (the "App", "we", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the App globally.

## 1. Scope

This Policy applies to your use of SnoreLog on iOS, Android, and other supported platforms, including recording, dB analysis, history management, export/share, trial features, and in-app purchases.

## 2. Information We Process

Based on the current implementation, we primarily process:

1. Microphone recording data  
Used for sleep recording and snore detection, stored as local audio files (such as `.m4a`).

2. Sound sample and statistical data  
Including dB samples, average/max dB, threshold, exceed count, start/end times, and duration for analysis and display.

3. Local app state data  
Including trial start time, local entitlement status (monthly/yearly/lifetime and expiry), interrupted-session draft data, and sample snapshots.

4. Permission state data  
Including microphone authorization status.

5. Payment-related data (processed by app stores)  
When you purchase or restore purchases, transaction processing is handled by Apple App Store / Google Play and related platforms. We only process necessary purchase result states to update local entitlements.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information only as necessary to provide core features, including:

1. Recording, playback, dB analysis, and history display;
2. Recovering interrupted recording sessions;
3. Determining trial and paid entitlement availability;
4. Supporting user-initiated export/share;
5. Maintaining app reliability and improving user experience.

## 4. Storage and Transfer

1. Local-first: recordings and analysis data are primarily stored on your device.  
2. The current version does not include built-in upload of recordings to our own backend.  
3. Product query, purchase, and restore flows communicate with app stores.  
4. If you choose export/share, files are transferred via the third-party app or service you select.

## 5. Permissions

The App currently requests:

1. Microphone permission: required for recording and real-time dB detection.

If denied, core recording features may be unavailable. You can change permissions at any time in system settings.

## 6. Third-Party SDKs / Services

Current integrations include (not limited to):

1. `in_app_purchase` / `in_app_purchase_storekit` for in-app purchases and restore;
2. `record` for audio recording and amplitude sampling;
3. `just_audio` for local audio playback;
4. `share_plus` for exporting/sharing recordings;
5. `shared_preferences`, `path_provider` for local storage and file path handling;
6. `permission_handler` for permission request/status checks.

Third parties may process data under their own privacy policies.

## 7. Retention and Deletion

1. Local recordings and analytics remain on your device until you delete recordings, clear app data, or uninstall.  
2. You can delete recordings in-app; deletion is generally irreversible.  
3. Uninstall and residual-data behavior depends on operating system rules.

## 8. Children

The App is not intended for children under the minimum age required by applicable law in their region. Minors should use the App with parent/guardian guidance.

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights such as access, correction, deletion, restriction/objection, and consent withdrawal (where applicable). You can also control permissions through your device settings.

## 10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

## 11. Policy Updates

We may update this Policy due to product, legal, or operational changes. Unless otherwise required by law, updates become effective upon publication.
