Welcome to MindInbox.

MindInbox helps you save, organize, and revisit links, notes, media, and other personal content. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we may share it, and what choices you have when using the service.

By using MindInbox, you understand and agree to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

### 1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use MindInbox, we may collect the following categories of information:

- Account information, such as your sign-in identifier, email address, display name, profile photo, and authentication method, including Google or Apple sign-in.
- Device and usage context, such as language, region, timezone, platform, installation identifier, push notification token, and limited diagnostic information required to operate the service.
- Content you choose to submit, save, or upload, including text, saved links, quoted content, folder names, images, videos, audio files, and other media.
- Subscription and entitlement information, such as whether paid features are active, whether a subscription is valid, and whether a purchase restore is needed. Full billing and payment details are generally handled by Apple, Google, or the relevant payment processor, not directly by MindInbox.
- Operational and log data, such as request logs, error reports, crash information, and security event records used to maintain reliability, security, and performance.

### 2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for purposes including:

- Creating and managing your account and sign-in session.
- Storing, organizing, syncing, and displaying your content.
- Sending notifications to devices you have authorized.
- Enabling subscription features, validating entitlements, restoring purchases, and enforcing free and paid plan rules.
- Detecting abnormal activity, preventing abuse, troubleshooting failures, and improving service quality.
- Improving product experience, refining features, and maintaining system reliability.

### 3. AI, Link Previews, and Automated Processing

When you save a supported public link, MindInbox may retrieve page metadata or publicly available content and use automated systems to generate summaries, previews, or related assistance.

In that process, the following may be handled:

- The link you save
- Public information associated with that link
- Related text you submit
- Context required to generate previews or summaries

Please note:

- AI-generated summaries, previews, or analysis are provided for convenience only and may be incomplete, delayed, or inaccurate.
- You should not submit confidential, highly sensitive, or regulated information unless you are comfortable with that processing.
- Even if an AI feature is temporarily unavailable, your content may still be stored without an AI-generated summary.

### 4. Sharing and Third-Party Service Providers

We do not sell your personal information.

To operate MindInbox, we may share necessary information with third-party service providers that help us provide the service, including but not limited to:

- Authentication providers, such as Firebase Authentication, Google Sign-In, and Sign in with Apple
- Push notification infrastructure, such as Firebase Cloud Messaging
- Backend, database, storage, and network providers, including Cloudflare-related services
- Subscription and entitlement infrastructure, such as RevenueCat and the relevant app store platform
- AI or content-processing providers used to generate summaries, previews, or other supported features when enabled

We may also disclose information when reasonably necessary:

- To comply with applicable law, regulation, court order, or legal process
- To enforce our terms or protect platform security
- To protect the rights, safety, or legitimate interests of users, developers, or the public
- To respond to valid and lawful government or judicial requests

### 5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

In general:

- Account, workspace, record, and folder data are kept until you delete the relevant content or delete your account.
- Uploaded images, videos, audio, and other media may remain stored while the related content remains active in the service.
- If you request account deletion through an in-app flow, we will attempt to remove your authentication access and associated backend account data.
- Some copies may remain temporarily in backups, caches, security logs, or recovery systems for a limited period before they are cleared under system policies.

### 6. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on the laws that apply where you live, you may have certain rights over your information. These may include the ability to:

- Decide whether to register for or sign in to MindInbox
- Choose which supported identity provider to use
- Manage permissions for photos, camera, microphone, and notifications through your device settings
- Delete content you have saved in the app
- Request account deletion through in-app tools when available
- Choose not to upload content you do not want processed

If you want to access, correct, or delete account-related data, you may contact us using the information below.
