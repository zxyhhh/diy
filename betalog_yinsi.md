# BetaLog Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BetaLog ("BetaLog", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use the BetaLog mobile app and related services. BetaLog is a climbing record app that helps users record climbing activity, import or capture climbing videos, manage gyms and route records, use AI analysis and Smart Scan features, back up media, and share selected route videos with climbing partners.

Privacy contact: [zxyhdream@gmail.com](mailto:zxyhdream@gmail.com)

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide, information generated when you use the app, and information needed to operate app features.

### Account Information

When you sign in, we may collect your user ID, Firebase UID, email address, display name, profile photo, sign-in provider, locale, time zone, and account status. If you update your profile, we may collect profile fields such as climbing age, height, highest grade, biography, avatar, climbing start date, and other profile details you choose to provide.

### Climbing Records and App Content

We collect climbing records you create or import, including dates, gyms, route names, route colors, grades, send or flash results, attempts, tags, notes, favorite status, completion status, manual records, clips, cover positions, statistics, saved gyms, hidden gyms, gym notes, upload preferences, and analysis status.

### Videos, Photos, Camera, Microphone, and Media

If you import, capture, upload, or analyze climbing media, BetaLog may access and process selected photo library items, camera output, microphone audio, video files, thumbnails, extracted frames, clip ranges, durations, file sizes, timestamps, local file paths, and related metadata.

Some video processing may happen on your device, such as compression, thumbnail generation, frame extraction, playback, and cache management. If you use upload, cloud backup, partner sharing, or AI analysis, relevant videos, thumbnails, frames, and metadata may be transmitted to our backend and service providers.

### AI Analysis and Smart Scan

When you use AI analysis or Smart Scan, BetaLog may extract selected frames from your climbing videos and send those frames, video title, duration, timestamps, and related metadata to our backend and AI processing providers. These features are used to help classify route attempts, identify send or flash results, organize records, and improve the app experience. AI output may be incomplete or inaccurate, so you should review and correct your own records.

### Location and Gym Data

If you grant location permission, BetaLog may use your approximate or precise location to show nearby climbing gyms, sort gyms by distance, and support gym search or selection. We may also store gyms you save, gym notes, hidden gyms, and gym-related route records.

### Partners, QR Codes, and Sharing

Partner features may process partner profiles, partner requests, relationship status, QR payloads, scanner results, shared route records, route mentions, selected videos, thumbnails, sender and receiver identifiers, and shared labels. When you send a partner request or share a route video, the recipient may see the information needed to view and respond to that request or shared record.

### Feedback and Support

If you submit feedback through the Feedback Wall, contact us, or participate in the BetaLog community, we may collect your feedback title, feedback body, status, timestamps, account identifiers, display name, email address, profile photo, and any information you choose to include. Feedback submitted to a public feedback feature may be visible to other users.

### Purchases and Subscription Status

If you purchase BetaLog Pro or restore purchases, we may process subscription status, entitlement status, product identifiers, purchase history, app user ID, and store-related purchase metadata through RevenueCat, Apple App Store, or Google Play. We do not receive your full payment card number.

### Device, Network, and Diagnostic Information

We may collect device and technical information needed to operate and secure the service, such as app version, operating system, device model, language settings, IP address, request logs, upload status, error messages, crash or diagnostic information, performance data, and service usage events.

## 2. Permissions We Request

BetaLog may request the following device permissions:

- Photo library or media access: to select, import, read, and manage climbing videos.
- Camera: to record climbing videos and scan partner QR codes.
- Microphone: to record audio when capturing videos.
- Location: to find nearby climbing gyms and sort gyms by distance.
- Network access: to sign in, sync records, upload media, run AI analysis, restore purchases, and submit feedback.
- App store billing: to purchase or restore BetaLog Pro.

You can manage device permissions in your system settings. Some features may not work if a required permission is disabled.

We may share information in the following cases:

## 6. Data Storage and Retention

We keep account information, climbing records, uploaded media, subscription status, feedback, and related service data while your account is active or as needed to provide BetaLog. We may also retain information as necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, maintain security, and enforce agreements.

Some data may be stored locally on your device for offline access, faster loading, media playback, upload queues, thumbnails, and cache. You can clear certain caches or remove local media through app settings or system storage controls.

Uploaded videos, thumbnails, route records, feedback, and account data may remain on our servers until you delete them where supported, request deletion, or the data is no longer needed for the purposes described in this policy. Backup copies and server logs may remain for a limited period after deletion.
