## KidLedger Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

### 1. Overview

KidLedger is designed to help families organize shared expenses, support payments, custody schedules, calendar events, documents, messages, reports, and notifications. This Policy explains what information the app may collect and how it may be used.

By using KidLedger, you understand that records you add to a family workspace may be visible to other authorized members of that workspace.

### 2. Information We Collect

- Account information: name, email address, sign-in provider identifiers, locale, and authentication-related data from Google or Apple.
- Family and ledger information: family membership, invite codes, children names, shared expenses, payments, support obligations, adjustments, recurring rules, balances, receipts, notes, and review status.
- Calendar and custody information: custody schedule settings, custody swap requests, calendar events, dates, times, participants, reasons, and event notes.
- Messages and documents: family discussion messages, expense messages, calendar or custody messages, uploaded document metadata, file names, file URLs, and related records.
- Device and usage information: push notification tokens, platform, app locale, request logs, diagnostics, and basic security or performance information.

### 3. How We Use Information

We use information to provide the app, authenticate users, maintain family workspaces, show shared records, calculate balances, send notifications, support exports and reports, troubleshoot issues, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.

We may use locale or device information to localize notifications and app behavior.

### 4. Sharing Information

Information may be shared with other users in your family workspace according to the app features. For example, a co-parent may see shared expenses, support records, calendar items, messages, documents, and review status.

We may share information with service providers that help operate the app, such as hosting, authentication, storage, notifications, analytics, or support providers, only as needed for those services.

We may disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, to investigate abuse, or in connection with a business transfer such as a merger or acquisition.

### 5. Third-Party Services

KidLedger may rely on third-party services such as Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In, Firebase Cloud Messaging, hosting providers, storage providers, and app platform services. Those services may process information under their own terms and privacy policies.

Your use of Google or Apple sign-in is also governed by the applicable provider's policies.

### 6. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain shared family records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and preserve audit history.

If you delete your account, some shared records may remain associated with the family workspace in a limited or de-identified form where needed for shared history, dispute context, or legal compliance.

### 7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No system is completely secure, and you should avoid uploading information that is more sensitive than necessary for family recordkeeping.

You are responsible for keeping your Google or Apple account secure and for limiting access to devices where you use KidLedger.

### 8. Your Choices

You can update certain profile and notification settings in the app. You may request account deletion through the account controls provided in the profile area.

You may disable push notifications through app settings or your device settings, though important account or service messages may still be provided in the app.

### 9. Children's Information

KidLedger may store children's names or child-related expense and calendar records entered by parents or guardians. The app is intended for adult account holders, not for direct use by children.

Do not add unnecessary sensitive information about a child. Use only what is needed for family recordkeeping.

### 10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact the KidLedger support channel provided in the app or by the account owner.
